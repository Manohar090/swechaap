Swecha was founded in 2005 as an organisation to support the Swecha project,it is now a vibrant community of software users, students, academicians and software professionals/developers determined to provide quality software built on the guidelines of free software development model.Swecha is part of Free Software Movement of India.


Swecha is one such platform where you can step up your career and experience innovation. It is a non-profit organisation which is aimed at the usage of free software technologies in the society. Swecha has launched its one month summer internship programme at VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology in asssociation with VNR IEEE Student Branch in order to provide quality software global solutions with free software development model. The intern programme was given in 3 streams, Internet of things(IOT), WEB DEVELOPMENT and DATA SCIENCE. The students who registered for the intern were taught with modern web, data science, HTML, pandas, python etc. They were introduced to different methodologies like Agile methodologies and exposed to different technologies. Apart from technical talks, it had impact sessions on GAFAM, AADHAR SURVEILLANCE, GENDER SENSITISATION AND FREE SOFTWARE and team building and collaboration activities too.
